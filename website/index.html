Heritage and Craftsmanship

An exceptional expertise

Coffee harvesting is a remarkable display of skilled craftsmanship. Workers meticulously hand-select the finest, perfectly ripe cherries, evaluating color, firmness, and maturity to capture the pinnacle of flavor. With multiple passes, they gather cherries at varying stages of ripeness, resulting in nuanced flavor profiles. Their deep understanding of coffee plants allows them to navigate the fields with precision, choosing cherries with ideal sweetness, acidity, and complexity. Beyond picking, these artisans contribute to quality control by carefully sorting and removing damaged or unripe cherries. Coffee harvesting demands physical agility, mental acuity, and an unwavering passion for flavor, bringing forth exceptional beans that delight coffee enthusiasts.
